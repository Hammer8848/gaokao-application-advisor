# Gaokao Application Checklist

Use this checklist for full志愿填报 support.

## Required User Inputs

- 省份
- 年份
- 科类 or 选科组合
- 高考总分
- 全省位次
- 批次线 or 特控线/本科线
- 志愿模式: 院校专业组, 专业+院校, 平行志愿, 顺序志愿, or province-specific model
- Target regions and excluded regions
- Preferred and excluded majors
- Budget and whether private/independent/Chinese-foreign cooperative programs are acceptable
- Career/postgraduate goals
- Employment preference: salary ceiling, stable employment,考公考编, technical career, medical/teacher/licensed profession, local employment, or global/large-city mobility
- Tolerance for industry volatility, overtime, graduate-degree dependency, and relocation
- Risk appetite

## Official Data To Verify

- 当前年招生计划
- 当前年招生章程
- 最近 2-3 年投档线 and最低位次
- 专业录取分/位次 when available
- 调剂 rules
- 体检 restrictions
- 单科成绩 requirements
- 外语语种 requirements
- 学费, campus, and住宿 location
- University graduate employment quality reports for the latest available year
- Major-level employment destination data when available
- Recent 3-5 year industry demand and salary reports
- Whether a major's good outcomes depend heavily on graduate school, licensing, or first-tier-city jobs

## Risk Checks

- 滑档 risk: too few保/兜底 choices
- 退档 risk: physical exam, single-subject, language, or major restriction mismatch
- 专业锁定 risk: school is acceptable only if调剂 leads to disliked majors
- 城市 mismatch: city preference conflicts with admission probability
- Data drift risk: using old rank data without current-year enrollment plan changes
- Cooperative-program risk: high tuition, separate major code, transfer restrictions, different campus or degree rules
- Salary illusion risk: using headline salary without checking city, degree, sample size, role type, and attrition
- Industry cycle risk: over-weighting a temporary hiring boom or under-weighting a current downturn
- Credential risk: recommending majors that require graduate school,执业资格,规培, or考证 without telling the user
- Major mismatch risk: recommending a high-pay major that conflicts with the student's interests, strengths, or tolerance for workload

## Output Checklist

The final answer should include:

- Assumptions and missing data
- Candidate table by冲稳保兜底
- Evidence year and rank basis
- For every school, recommended major(s) or院校专业组 and why those majors fit
- Employment/salary/industry view for each recommended major
- Major/charter risks
- What to verify before submission
- A short explanation of tradeoffs
